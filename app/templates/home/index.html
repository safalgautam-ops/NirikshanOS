Have questions? Review the platform essentials.

What forensic capabilities are included?

NirikshanOS supports tools such as ExifTool, Sleuth Kit, Volatility3, Plaso, capa, Foremost, and Scalpel. Available modules and worker sizes depend on the organization's active plan.

How is evidence integrity protected?

Evidence uploads stream to S3-compatible object storage and receive automatic SHA-256 and MD5 hashes. Case activity is audit logged for chain-of-custody review.

How does organization access work?

Organizations pass an approval workflow, then owners can delegate organization-scoped roles and permissions. Case visibility remains limited to owners, creators, and explicitly assigned members.

How is forensic analysis isolated?

Jobs run in ephemeral Docker containers selected from light, medium, heavy, or full worker classes. Raw stdout and stderr are preserved without a lossy parsing layer.

Can teams build investigation reports?

Yes. Analysts can save findings and indicators, reconstruct events in the case timeline, and insert those records into a markdown report.

How do subscriptions and payments work?

Active plans are managed from the organization Billing page. Free plans activate directly, while paid monthly or annual plans use server-verified eSewa payments with coupon and organization discount support.
